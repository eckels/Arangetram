We are excited to announce Divya's Bharatanatyam Arangetram!

Bharatanatyam is an ancient dance form that originated in the temples of Southern India. This dance form is known for its grace, elegance, purity of expression and sculpturesque poses. It is an incredibly difficult and beautiful dance form that combines intense footwork with vibrant emotions.

The Arangetram is a dancer's debut for solo performance and is the culmination of years of training, dedication and practice. The dancer is traditionally accompanied by an orchestra and performs his or her repertoire to live music.

Divya will be dancing for a total of about 2 hours, dressed up in the traditional costume with intricate hair and ornate jewelry. She has been working towards this specific performance since December.
